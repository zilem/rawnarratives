Inspired by Ray Dalio’s Principles, here are a set of concisely stated principles that collaborators can count on when working with me.

PRINCIPLES

For making decisions at Work.

1. Determine Why… we do the things we do. Not just how or what.

2. Generate Plenty… of ideas. In order to get a few home runs.

3. Purposeful Curiosity… is about the genuine inquiry to listen, learn and empathize.

4. Non-Judgmental… is to avoid developing preconceived biases toward others’ opinions.

5. Self-Determination… is autonomy; the willingness to reflect on different opinions.

__________

6. Storify… wherever possible. A journey about wants, needs, stakes and obstacles.

7. Emotion over Logic… solutions that appeal to the heart over engaging the mind.

8. Authentic Appeal… is being authentic to ourselves whilst appealing to the audience.

9. Iterative Rigour… involves revisiting and refining repeatedly till satisfaction or deadline.

10. Brand Alignment… from big picture to smallest detail singing the same tune.

__________

11. Mindset over Experience
willingness to solve issues external to domain of expertise.

12. Embrace Uncertainty
and manage expectations of customized untested solutions.

13. Go Slowly
when faced with two seemingly mutually exclusive choices.

14. Seek Counsel
of mentors and wizards to illuminate blind spots.

15. Done is better than Perfect
perfection is the objective but never the destination.
