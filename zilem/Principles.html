Inspired by Ray Dalio’s hugely popular book Principles, here are a set of concisely stated principles that clients and collaborators can count on when working with me.

They provide a good indication of whether the collaboration would be a good fit for both parties.

PRINCIPLES

That guide Decision-Making at Work.


1.
Determine Why
we do the things we do. Not just how or what.


2.
Purposeful Curiosity
is the genuine inquiry to listen, learn and empathize.


3.
Being Non-Judgmental
is avoiding preconceived biases toward others’ opinions.


4.
Need over Want
we know what we want. It's more important to figure out what we need.


5.
Storifying
is creating a journey about wants, needs, stakes and obstacles.

__________


6.
Authentic Appeal
is being authentic to ourselves whilst appealing to the audience.


7.
Emotion over Logic
solutions that appeal to the heart over engaging the mind.


8.
Simplicity for Clarity
the core idea or benefit of any product must be easily stated and understood.


9.
Brand Alignment
from big picture to smallest detail singing the same tune.


10.
Seek Counsel
of mentors and wizards to illuminate blind spots.
