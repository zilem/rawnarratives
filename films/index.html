Films

Outsider stories that inspire self-actualization

FOR THE PAST four years, I've been knee-deep in two screenplays based on true stories - real-life individuals who derive an extraordinary sense of purpose pursuing compelling causes that empower their communities.

Over the next five years, I'll be collaborating with global professionals, who apart from bringing these films to the big screen, would derive meaning from a social cause they believe in.

The team has already begun to form. If these issues interest you, I look forward to our conversation.

Have you ever grieved over the undue loss of your loved one so badly, your life's mission is dedicated to found a social cause?


Sheepwolf

An epic journey of survival from abuse, poverty & discrimination

A TRUE STORY. After surviving childhood abuse and racial discrimination, a single father abandons his corporate career to pursue his calling, founding Southeast Asia’s largest self-defense movement to protect and empower women.

The screenplay is a finalist in the Evolution! Mallorca Film Festival, a semi-finalist in the Creative World Awards and a quarter-finalist in the Los Angeles Chinese Film Festival.


S H A R E        

Garage Spacemen

The attempt is more significant than the outcome

THE INCREDIBLE TRUE story about a Singaporean maverick scientist and his team behind the World’s first volunteer-driven mission to send a man to space in a garage spacecraft.

Waiting for Sunrise

A love story about guilt & existential crises

FOLLOWING HIS DIVORCE, a suicidal vampire begins a love affair with an old friend. When a terminal illness befalls her, he struggles to reconcile her remaining days with the curse of his infinitely mundane existence.
