raw Mission

A Manifesto in 3 Acts.

TO MOVE & INSPIRE the inquiring everyman with stories that ignite the human spirit.










raw Vision

Finding purpose in an A.I. driven world.

FOR ALL HUMAN beings to discover and act on their life's calling.










raw Values

Core to my Moral Compass.


Authenticity
Genuine appreciation for existential struggles.


Inclusivity
Empathy for outsiders.


Autonomy
Freedom of artistic self-expression.










raw Principles

That guide decision-making at Work.


1.
Serve the Audience
Listen. Validate. Give them what they need over what they want.


2.
Determine Why
Why we do the things we do. Not just how or what.


3.
Authentic Appeal
Being authentic to ourselves whilst appealing to the audience.


4.
Emotion over Logic
Solutions that appeal to the heart over engaging the mind.


5.
Fulfillment over Achievement
The pursuit of meaning transcends serving the ego.
